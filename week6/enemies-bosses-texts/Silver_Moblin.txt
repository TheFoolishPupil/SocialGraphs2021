"Silver Moblins are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.[name reference needed]\n\n\n== Characteristics ==\n\n\n=== Breath of the Wild ===\n\nSilver Moblins are tall monsters that can be found all over Hyrule Kingdom. Due to Calamity Ganon's magic, they are a powerful variant of Moblin, surpassing even Black Moblins, but are outclassed by Golden Moblins. They are armed with the strongest Moblin Weapons.[which?] When defeated, they may drop Moblin Horns, Moblin Fangs, Moblin Guts, Amber, Opal, Topaz, Rubies, Sapphires, and Diamonds.\n\n\n== Other Appearances ==\n\n\n=== Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity ===\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== See Also ==\nMoblin\nBlack Moblin\nBlue Moblin\nElectric Moblin\nFire Moblin\nGolden Moblin\nIce Moblin\nStalmoblin\n\n\n== References =="
