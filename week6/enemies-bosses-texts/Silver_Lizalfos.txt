"Silver Lizalfos are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.\n\n\n== Characteristics ==\n\n\n=== Breath of the Wild ===\n\nSilver Lizalfos are a strong variant of Lizalfos, surpassing both Blue Lizalfos and Black Lizalfos due to the influence of Calamity Ganon's dark magic. They have a high health of 864 and carry the strongest variants of Lizalfos Weapons. They attack Link by hitting him, spitting streams of water, and striking him with their tongue. Silver Lizalfos are capable of swimming and spitting water at Link from the water. Link can defeat Silver Lizalfos with any Weapon or Bow. When defeated, Silver Lizalfos may drop Lizalfos Horns, Lizalfos Talons, Lizalfos Tails, Amber, Opal, Rubies, Sapphires, and Diamonds.\n\n\n== Other Appearances ==\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== See Also ==\nBlue Lizalfos\nBlack Lizalfos\nCursed Lizalfos\nGolden Lizalfos\nFire-Breath Lizalfos\nIce-Breath Lizalfos\nElectric Lizalfos\nStalizalfos\n\n\n== References =="
