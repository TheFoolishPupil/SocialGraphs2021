"Bamboo is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nBamboo is a Horse belonging to Baumar, a Hylian traveler. The two of them travel the path between Serenne Stable and Snowfield Stable so that Baumar can go Shield Surfing in the Hebra Mountains.\nLike many Horses, Bamboo is outfitted with the standard Stable Bridle and Stable Saddle. Baumar shows a lot of affection for Bamboo, referring to them as \"totally gnarly\", \"radical\", and \"most excellent\".\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== See Also ==\nHorse\nSerenne Stable\nSnowfield Stable\nBaumar\n\n\n== References =="
