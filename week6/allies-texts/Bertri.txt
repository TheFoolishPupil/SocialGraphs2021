"Bertri is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nBertri is a Gerudo soldier who can be found in the palace in Gerudo Town. She guards one of the entrances to Riju's room upstairs. If Link tries to go past her before recovering the Thunder Helm from the Yiga Clan Hideout, she stops him and tells him that he better leave if he does not want to be skewered. After recovering the Thunder Helm, Bertri is shocked that Link has permission to enter Riju's bedroom and asks who he is.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== See Also ==\nGerudo Town\nKatta\n\n\n== References =="
