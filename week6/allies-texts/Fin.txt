"Fin is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nFin is a member of the Shield Breakers that camps out near Woodland Stable. Upon talking to him, he asks Link if he is up for \"shield-surfing\". If Link agrees to try out shield-surfing, Fin points him to his best friend, Russ, under a tree on top of the hill.\nAfter reaching the top of the hill, Russ apologizes to Link for Fin pressuring him into shield-surfing, and offers him shields to buy.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
