"Gaddison is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\n\nGaddison is a guard for Zora's Domain who can be found guarding the eastern bridge, wielding a Zora Spear. When first approached by Link, she will recognize him, claiming they played together 100 years ago. She refrains from asking him too many questions since she's on duty.\nIn her youth, Gaddison was part of the Big Bad Bazz Brigade with Bazz and Rivan.\nAs part of the \"From the Ground Up\" Side Quest, Gaddison can be asked to move to Tarrey Town due to her name ending in \"-son\". However, she will refuse Link's request.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
