"Pepp is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nPepp can be found inside the Great Deku Tree, specifically inside the Navel, in the Korok Forest.  Pepp made a bed of leaves for Link, where Link can sleep for free to pass time or recover hearts.  Pepp often falls asleep while waiting for Link.\n\n\n== Trivia ==\nPepp's leaf mask and appearance resemble Aldo from The Wind Waker.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
