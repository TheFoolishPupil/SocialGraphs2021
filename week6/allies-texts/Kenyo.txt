"Kenyo is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\n\nKenyo can be found at Tabantha Bridge Stable, near Tabantha Great Bridge. He tells Link of the \"Boom-Bam Ghost\", and is shocked to find that Link does not know what he is talking about. He goes on to explain that under the Tabantha Great Bridge, there is a ghost that creates loud noises.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
