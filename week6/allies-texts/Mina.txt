"Mina is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nMina is a Hylian who hunts for treasure with her brother Mils. The siblings can be found near the Exchange Ruins north of the Great Plateau. The two of them can be attacked by Bokoblins.\nMina demands that the siblings continue to search for treasure even when Mils wishes to return home.\nMina mistakes Link for a treasure hunter and advises him to avoid Hyrule Castle.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== See Also ==\nMils\n\n\n== References =="
