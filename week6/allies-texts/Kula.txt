"Kula is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nKula can be found wandering around near The Great Deku Tree in Korok Forest. Upon speaking to them, they welcome Link to the forest enthusiastically. They offer Link a trial, the Side Quest \"A Freezing Rod\", in which he must show them an Ice Rod.\nAfter showing Kula the Ice Rod, they are extremely impressed. As thanks, they give Link 100 Rupees.\nIf shown a Blizzard Rod Kula will instead give Link 300 Rupees.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
