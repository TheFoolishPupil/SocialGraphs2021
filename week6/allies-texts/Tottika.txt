"Tottika is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\n\nTottika is a guard for Zora's Domain who can be seen holding a Silverscale Spear.\nHe is a new recruit. He tells Link about the existence of the Master Sword, and then adds that he wishes to become a great warrior worthy of being chosen by a weapon.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
