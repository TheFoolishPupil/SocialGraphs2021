"Rik is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nRik is a Hylian traveler who makes his way between Wetland Stable and Kakariko Village. He is something of a worrywart, often worrying about people he knows and fellow travelers such as Link. Since he frequents Kakariko Village, he is aware of the feud between the Sheikah farmers Steen and Olkin, expressing concern about the conflict.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
