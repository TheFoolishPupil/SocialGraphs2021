"Tona is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\n\nBefore Link travels to Zora's Domain for the first time, Tona finds Link at the base of Zora River near Lanayru Tower. She is swimming in the water and calls out to him. She encourages Link to meet Sidon at Inogo Bridge upstream.\nIn Zora's Domain, she can be found on the second level chatting with Tula and Laruta about Sidon.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\nTona's name is derived from tone.\n\n\n== References =="
