"Gartan is a character in Breath of the Wild.[citation needed]\n\n\n== Biography ==\nGartan can be found traveling around on foot in the Gerudo Desert, selling his wares. He enjoys being a merchant because it gives him an excuse to talk to the Gerudo. Gartan can also be found at the Gerudo Canyon Stable, where he tells Link that he wishes more Gerudo ladies would come to the Stable. Gartan sometimes mutters about his determination to get into Gerudo Town and get close to the ladies. When Gartan notices Link listening, he tells Link that he was just muttering about \"important business secrets.\" While Link speaks to Gartan at the Kara Kara Bazaar, Gartan is excited about making small talk with the Gerudo there. Gartan then says that it is just part of his job to make friends and asks Link if he wants some Apples.\nIf Link speaks to Gartan near Gerudo Town, he catches him muttering to himself that there must be a way into the Town.
