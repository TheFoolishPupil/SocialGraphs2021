"Guardian Scouts I are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.[name reference needed]\n\n\n== Characteristics ==\n\n\n=== Breath of the Wild ===\nGuardian Scout I can be found in shrines that involve fighting. While other Guardians were made to combat Calamity Ganon, Guardian Scouts were made specifically for Ancient Shrines. They are the weakest of all the Guardian Scout, and have no ancient weaponry at their disposal. They only use lasers to fight. These types of Guardians can be found in the Oman Au Shrine, the Keh Namut Shrine, and other Ancient Shrines.
