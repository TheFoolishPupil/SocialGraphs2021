"Fyson is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nFyson is a Rito who lives in Rito Village. He helps his mother with her shop, but he wants to open his own. However, he does not want to compete with his mother's shop, so he is found searching for a new place to open it. During the \"From the Ground Up\" Side Quest, after asking for 30 pieces of Wood to build more homes, Hudson will ask Link to find someone who would like to open a general store. Once informed, Fyson will leave Rito Village and open the Slippery Falcon Tarrey Town Branch. In it, he sells Ancient Gear as well as bundles of every type of Arrow other than Ancient Arrows.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
