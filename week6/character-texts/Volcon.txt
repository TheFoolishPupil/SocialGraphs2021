"Volcon is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nVolcon is a Goron who stands outside of the Rollin' Inn in Goron City, where he directs travelers to the lodge. Volcon helps run the Inn alongside Tray, and gives Goron-style massages to its customers.\nAfter the Magma Bombs stop falling following the Divine Beast Vah Rudania's liberation, Volcon becomes hopeful that droves of tourists will start coming to Goron City and stay at the Inn.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
