"Monari is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nMonari is an elderly woman who lives in Tarrey Town on Lake Akkala. Upon first meeting Monari, she mentions strange plants and guys with ugly faces that live outside the village. She also mentions a big flower southeast of Tarrey Town. Monari is married to Moggs, who also lives in Tarrey Town.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
