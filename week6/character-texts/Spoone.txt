"Spoone is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\n\nSpoone is a doctor that travels between Wetland Stable and Riverside Stable in search of patients in need. He is interested in Naydra's Scale for its healing capabilities. Spoone tells Link that one can find Naydra's Scale at the Spring of Wisdom, along with Naydra.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
