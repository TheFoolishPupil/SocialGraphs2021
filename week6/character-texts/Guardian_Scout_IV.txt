"Guardian Scouts IV are enemies in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Characteristics ==\n\nThe Guardian Scout IV is the strongest variation of the Guardian Scout, found within \"A Major Test of Strength\" Ancient Shrines. In order to complete these shrines, Link must defeat the Guardian Scouts IV inside. Being the most upgraded versions, Guardian Scouts IV are capable of wielding three weapons at once.
