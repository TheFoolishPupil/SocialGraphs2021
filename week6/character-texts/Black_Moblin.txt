"Black Moblins are recurring Enemies in The Legend of Zelda series.\n\n\n== Characteristics ==\n\n\n=== Breath of the Wild ===\n\nBlack Moblins are tall, heavy monsters that can be found all over Hyrule. They are stronger, more resilient variants of Blue Moblins. They are usually armed with the strongest Moblin Weapons.
