"Dauntless is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nDauntless is a horse belonging to Nazbi, a Hylian treasure hunter. Together they can be found traveling around Serenne Stable, the Tabantha Bridge Stable, and Snowfield Stable.\nNazbi claims to have become dependent upon Dauntless, being unable to run a few steps on his own without getting winded. Nazbi shows a great deal of affection toward Dauntless, who he describes as his \"beloved Dauntless,\" \"dear horse,\" \"noble steed,\" and \"equal partner.\"\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
