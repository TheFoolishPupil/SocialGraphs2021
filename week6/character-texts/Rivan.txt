"Rivan is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nRivan is a Zora who wields a Silverscale Spear and stands guard at the entrance of Zora's Domain with is daughter, Dunma. He is the son of Trello, whom he warns Link against seeing due to the prejudice older Zora have against Hylians. He is over 130 years old.\nWhen Link first encounters him, Rivan will reveal that they used to swim together when he was a child at the age of 30. During this time he was friends with Bazz and Gaddison, the three of them forming the Big Bad Bazz Brigade.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== References =="
