"This article is about the Sheikah Monk. For the Ancient Shrine named after this character, see Zuna Kai Shrine.\n\nZuna Kai is a character in Breath of the Wild.\n\n\n== Biography ==\nZuna Kai is a Sheikah Monk who serves the Goddess Hylia. Following the first Great Calamity and the ostracism of the Sheikah 10,000 years prior to Breath of the Wild, Zuna Kai became a Monk and constructed an Ancient Shrine to help prepare Link against the threat of Calamity Ganon.\nFollowing Link's Slumber of Restoration in the Shrine of Resurrection, the Zuna Kai Shrine is activated and can then be entered by someone bearing a Sheikah Slate. When Link enters the Shrine, he is greeted by the Monk and is offered a blessing for overcoming the ordeal of entering the Shrine. If Link speaks to the Monk, Zuna Kai praises him and rewards the Hylian Champion with a Spirit Orb. With their role fulfilled, Zuna Kai wishes Link well as their body fades away.\n\n\n== Nomenclature ==\n\n\n== See Also ==\nZuna Kai Shrine\n\n\n== References =="
